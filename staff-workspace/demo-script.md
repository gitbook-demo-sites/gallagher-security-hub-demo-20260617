# Demo script

## Suggested flow

1. Start as an end user and show the public-first Security Hub.
2. Switch to Channel Partner and show commercial partner overlays.
3. Switch to High Security Channel Partner and show restricted high security content.
4. Switch to Technology Partner and show SDK onboarding.
5. Switch to CP plus Tech Partner to show simultaneous access.
6. Switch to staff and show full access plus internal governance.
