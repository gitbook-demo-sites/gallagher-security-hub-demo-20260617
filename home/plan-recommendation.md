# Plan recommendation

For this scope, the recommended tier is Enterprise || the highest site tier available to the account.

## Why

- Adaptive content for multiple authenticated audiences.
- Visitor Authentication for partner, technology partner, and staff access.
- Advanced site customization and branded navigation.
- AI Assistant over a large knowledge base.
- Git Sync for migration, review, and long-term content operations.
- Localization workflow with Crowdin and structured source content.
- Governance features for a high-volume migration from MindTouch.

## Commercial assumption

This demo assumes Gallagher wants one public-first knowledge platform that still supports controlled access for three gated content areas. If the team instead wants fully separate legal || regional publishing boundaries, the site architecture should be revisited before implementation.
